Glossary

*Make sure to consult a nutritionist for your personal dietary needs. The recipes generated are suggestions based on a 2,000 calorie DV.

Daily Value: This is the recommended amount of calories you need per day to function. It varies based on age, height, activity, etc. and can be calculated using online calculators. However, consult a nutritionist for accurate details.

Protein: Large, complex molecules that drive chemical processes in your body. The average sedentary adult should consume around 0.8g protein/kg body weight a day.

Carbohydrates (carbs): Sugar molecules that are broken down into glucose. On average, people should be getting 45%-65% of their calories from carbs, total.

Fats: Nutrients that build cell membranes, the brain, and hormones. There are three types of fat, and the average adult should consume 30% of their DV from unsaturated fats, with no more than 5%-6% being from saturated fats.

Allergens: Make sure to list potential allergies you may have to certain ingredients, like peanuts, shellfish, or milk, so that Sammy knows to avoid them!
